# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

Jeans Ruiz ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ShadowingKit mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use the App, we may collect the following personal information:

**Account Information:**
- Apple ID (used for Sign in with Apple authentication)
- Unique user identifier generated by Apple
- Email address (optional, provided through Sign in with Apple)

**Subscription Information:**
- Subscription status (free, trial, or premium)
- Subscription plan type (monthly or annual)
- Subscription purchase and renewal dates
- Payment transaction identifiers (processed by Apple, we do not store payment card details)

**User-Generated Content:**
- Audio recordings you create within the App
- Audio and video files you import into the App
- Transcripts generated from your audio content
- Text notes or preferences you save
- Content you mark as favorites

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

**Usage Data:**
- App features you use and how you interact with them
- Time spent on different screens and activities
- Content you access, view, or practice with
- Episode completion rates and progress tracking
- Settings and preferences

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version (iOS)
- App version
- Unique device identifiers
- Language settings

**Performance Data:**
- Crash reports and error logs
- App performance metrics
- Diagnostic information

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information about you:

**RevenueCat:**
- Subscription status and purchase history
- Customer identifiers linking your Apple ID to our user database
- Trial eligibility status

**Amplitude & Firebase:**
- Analytics data about your app usage
- Aggregated behavioral patterns
- Performance and crash analytics

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To create and manage your account

### 2.2 Subscription Management

- To process and manage your subscription purchases
- To verify your premium access entitlements
- To provide customer support for billing issues
- To send subscription-related notifications (e.g., trial expiration, renewal reminders)

### 2.3 Analytics and Research

- To understand how users interact with the App
- To identify areas for improvement and new feature development
- To analyze learning patterns and content effectiveness
- To measure app performance and stability

### 2.4 Communications

- To respond to your inquiries and support requests
- To send important updates about the App or our policies
- To notify you of significant changes to features or terms

### 2.5 Legal Compliance

- To comply with legal obligations and regulations
- To protect our rights and prevent fraud or abuse
- To enforce our Terms and Conditions

### 3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
- Protect and defend our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or illegal activity

### 3.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice in the App before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by:
- **Supabase:** User authentication, profiles, and metadata
- **AWS S3:** User-generated audio and video content

These services use industry-standard security measures and are located in secure data centers with redundant backups.

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of data at rest for sensitive information
- Secure authentication using Apple's Sign in with Apple
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls limiting who can access your data

### 4.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

**Account Data:** Retained until you delete your account
**User-Generated Content:** Retained until you delete the content or your account
**Usage Analytics:** Retained in aggregated form for up to 2 years
**Subscription History:** Retained for tax and legal compliance purposes (typically 7 years)

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete:
- Your user profile and preferences
- All user-generated content (recordings, imports, favorites)
- Personal identifiers linking you to the App

Some information may be retained in backup systems for a limited time and in aggregated, anonymized form for analytics.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your information through the App:
- View your account details and subscription status
- Manage your content and favorites
- Update preferences and settings

### 5.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your data:
- **Delete Content:** You can delete individual recordings or imported content at any time
- **Delete Account:** You can delete your entire account through the App's Settings, which will permanently remove all your personal data and content

### 5.3 Subscription Management

You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID:
- View subscription status and renewal date
- Cancel auto-renewal
- Change subscription plans
- View payment history

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iOS device.

### 5.4 Marketing Communications

We currently do not send marketing communications. If we introduce marketing emails in the future, you will be able to opt out at any time.

### 5.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at shadowifyapp@gmail.com. We will provide your data in a structured, commonly used format.

### 5.6 Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent for data processing by:
- Adjusting app permissions in your device settings
- Deleting your account
- Uninstalling the App

Note that withdrawing consent may limit your ability to use certain App features.

## 6. Age Restrictions and Privacy

The App is intended for users 18 years of age and older only. The App is rated 18+ in the Apple App Store, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

**If you are under 18 years of age:**
- You are not permitted to use the App
- Do not create an account or provide any personal information
- Do not use any features of the App

**For Parents and Guardians:**
If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 18 has used the App or provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at shadowifyapp@gmail.com. We will:
- Delete all personal information associated with the account
- Permanently terminate the account
- Remove any user-generated content created by the minor

We take age restrictions seriously and will take prompt action to remove any accounts or data belonging to users under 18.
